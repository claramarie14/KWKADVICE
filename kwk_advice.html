WHAT IS KWK?

KODE WITH KLOSSY

The Kode with Klossy program focuses on hands-on learning through labs and demos. Scholars gain skills and proficiency in both web development and programming through building their own web apps. Working together in teams, collaborating with inspiring instructors and fellow girls to explore the incredible world of code. The scholarship is open to girls age 13-18 and you do not need any previous coding experience to apply! Scholars learn valuable coding skills and professional software engineering tools. Karlie Kloss aims to support and inspire girls to realize their full potential and learn this really empowering life skill.
